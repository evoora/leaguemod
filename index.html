Frequently Asked Questions

What is LeagueMod?

LeagueMod is a tool that simplifies queuing, picks, and bans in League of Legends while ensuring security with Riot’s LCU API. It also integrates with OP.GG and allows for client customization with unique themes.

How does the automated queuing feature work?

The automated queuing feature streamlines the game setup process by automatically handling champion selection and queuing, saving you time and effort during the pre-game phase.

Is LeagueMod safe to use with my League of Legends account?

Yes, LeagueMod is designed to work securely with Riot’s LCU API, ensuring that your account remains safe while using the tool's features.

Can I customize the client’s appearance with LeagueMod?

Yes, LeagueMod allows you to personalize your League of Legends client with custom themes, enhancing your gaming experience with a unique visual style.

What platforms are supported by LeagueMod?

LeagueMod is designed to work with the Windows version of League of Legends. Support for other platforms may be added in the future.

How do I get updates for LeagueMod?

Updates for LeagueMod are automatically handled through the tool. You will be notified when a new update is available, ensuring you always have the latest features and improvements.

Is there a way to integrate LeagueMod with OP.GG?

Yes, LeagueMod seamlessly integrates with OP.GG, providing enhanced game analysis and insights directly within the client.
